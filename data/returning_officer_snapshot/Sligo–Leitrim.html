NOTICE OF DÁIL ELECTION / FÓGRA TOGHCHÁIN DÁLA

Applications for Work

We are now accepting applications for work on the General / Dáil Election.  You can apply by clicking on "Apply for Work in Sligo" or "Apply for Work in Leitrim" in the menu at the left hand side of this web page.

We will ONLY accept applications made through this website so please do not phone or write to us looking for work.

Persons campaigning for any candidate in the election are NOT ELIGIBLE to work for us on this election.
