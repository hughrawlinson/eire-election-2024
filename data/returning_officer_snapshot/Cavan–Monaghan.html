Application for Work

  • Print

We are currently accepting applications for work at the General Election being held on 29th November 2024.    You may apply to work in either Cavan or Monaghan by clicking the "Work in Cavan" OR "Work in Monaghan" items in the menu on the left.

N.B. If you are canvasing for any party or candidate in any election that may be held on the that day, you are NOT ELIGIBLE to work on this occasion.
