NOTICE OF POLL

DÁIL GENERAL ELECTION 2024
CONSTITUENCY OF WICKLOW-WEXFORD

Take Notice that, pursuant to a writ issued to me by the Clerk of Dáil Éireann, I will take a poll on 29th November, 2024, between the hours of 07:00am and 10:00pm to elect 3 members of Dáil Éireann for this constituency.

The names and descriptions of the candidates standing nominated and of their proposers (if any), and the order in which they will appear on the ballot paper, are set out hereunder.

BOLANDAONTÚ
(Sinéad Boland, of Granite Field, Rocky Valley, Kilmacanogue, Co. Wicklow
Former Teacher)

 
BRENNANFINE GAEL
(Brian Brennan, of Upper Gorey Hill, Gorey, Co. Wexford
Hospitality Consultant)

 
BYRNEFIANNA FÁIL
(Malcolm Byrne, of The Chase, Gorey , Co. Wexford
Public Representative)

 
KENNEDYFIANNA FÁIL
(Pat Kennedy, of Kirikee, Greenane, Rathdrum, Co. Wicklow
Public Servant)

 
LAWLORINDEPENDENT IRELAND
(Frances Lawlor, of Charlesland, Greystones, Co. Wicklow
Homemaker)

 
LEONARDNON PARTY
(Peir Leonard, of South Winds, South Quay, Arklow, Co. Wicklow
County Councillor)

 
NOLANNON-PARTY
(Ilse-Maria Nolan, of Cabinteely, Dublin 18
Director - Medical Company)

 
O'KEEFFEPEOPLE BEFORE PROFIT - SOLIDARITY
(Aislinn O'Keeffe, of C/O Solidarity, 141 Thomas Street, Dublin 8
Tour Guide)

Proposed By: Deirdre Tuohy of Monaseed, Gorey, Co. Wexford
SÚILLEABHÁINSINN FÉIN
(Fionntán Ó, Súilleabháin, of Cnoc Bhaile Thomáis, Guaire, Co. Loch Garman
Public Representative)

 
WALSHGREEN PARTY/COMHAONTAS GLAS
(Ann Walsh, of Castletown, Inch, Co. Wexford
Psychologist)
