Inaccessible Polling Station

PUBLIC NOTICE

Dáil Elections – Carlow Kilkenny

Polling Day 29th November 2024

TAKE NOTICE: that the following Polling Station is not readily accessible to wheelchair users:

Freshford National School, Freshford Lots, Freshford, Co. Kilkenny


Voters with a physical illness or physical disability, whose name is not on the postal or special voters list, and who may have difficulty gaining access to their local polling station may apply to have their vote transferred to a more accessible polling station in their constituency. This facility is intended to assist voters who find access to their local polling station difficult.
In order to avail of the facility, voters should apply to the returning officer by Friday 22nd November 2024 at the latest.
A special application form is not necessary. If possible, voters should suggest an alternative polling station in the constituency which is both suitable and convenient to them.

Working at Elections

We are now accepting applications for work on the General Election being held on 29th November 2024.  You can apply by clicking on either "Work in Carlow" OR "Work in Kilkenny" in the menu at the left hand side of this web page.

We will ONLY accept applications made through this website so please do not phone or write to us looking for work.

Persons campaigning for any party or candidate in the election are NOT ELIGIBLE to work for us on this occassion.  This includes posts made on social media.
