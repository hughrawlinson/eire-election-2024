Media Notice

GENERAL ELECTION 2024
29 NOVEMBER 2024

ARRANGEMENTS FOR POLL AND COUNTING OF VOTES


Dear Editor
 
I write to advise you of arrangements for the forthcoming poll and counting of votes.

  • The poll will be taken between 7.00 a.m. and 10.00 p.m. on Friday, 29TH November.
  • At the close of poll, ballot boxes will be delivered to the count centre in Loreto Secondary School, Sports Hall, Ballynagee, Wexford.

What constituency am I in?

The constituency boundaries have changed since the last general election and in particular there is a new constituency of Wicklow-Wexford which contains portions of those two counties.    The Electoral Commission have published a webpage which will let you check what constituency you are registered in which you may find helpful.

Applications for work

We are currently accepting applications for work at the next General Election being held on 29th November 2024.  You can submit an application by clicking on "Apply for work" in the menu at the top left hand side of this webpage.

NB: If you are campaigning for any party or candidate, in the election, then you are not eligible to work on this occassion.   This includes posts made on social media.
